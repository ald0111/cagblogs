Mosquitoes


Zing! Zang! Zung! Zoon!

Zingy wing and zangy wang
And Zungy wung and zoon,
Husky young mosquitoes
‘Neath Summer moon.

Zingy play’d the ukulele,
Zangy play’d guitar,
Wung and zoon with singing
Woke echoes far

They sang and thrummed and play’d until
The dawn grew gray o’erhead,
Then one mosquito, thoughtfully
Unto the others said:

There’s going to be picnic
Out in the park, to-night, to-night,
There’s going to be a picnic
Out in the park,
We better sharpen up, sharpen up!

Zoon! Zung! Zang! Zing!
